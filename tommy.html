Power Rangers: Dino Thunder Tommy Oliver

As one of the longest lasting active Power Ranger throughout the entire series, Dr. Thomas Oliver aka "Tommy". After passing his title of Red Turbo Ranger to finish his education, he discovered the Dino Gems

During this time he became a teacher at Reefside High School where after a fateful day at detention with Conner, Ethan, and Kira his role expands to become their mentor as a veteran Power Ranger.
