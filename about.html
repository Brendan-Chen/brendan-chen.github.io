A little about the arist behind the camera

Why do I take photos?

As a hobbyist photographer I spend my time taking because at first the landscape wallpapers were not what I was looking for. Being a creature of habit, I have a tendency to keep the same wallpapers year round, so if it is something that I will look at everyday I want it to be mine. And so started my journey into photography.
