What caused the formation of the group?

One fateful day, three teenagers attending reefside high are all sent to detention with Professor Tommy Oliver. Little do they know that Tommy Oliver is a Power Ranger veteran and that the three teens, Connor McKnight, Ethan James, and Kira Ford are about to become the next generation of Power Rangers.

Conner McKnight

Conner was a star soccer player at Reefside High School where he somewhat embraces the stereotype of a "dumb jock" but is a natural born leader.

After his interaction with the red dino gem, Connor obtained the abilities of T-rex speed. This gave him the power to move and react at superhuman speeds much like a supehero speedster you would see in comics.

Ethan James

Ethan James is a skilled hacker and computer expert at Reefside High School, found pulling pranks such as turning on the schools automated sprinkler system during school hours.

After his interaction with the blue dino gem, Ethan obtained the abilities of Tricera skin. This gave him the power to make his skin impenetrable much like the scales of a Triceratops.

Kira Ford

Kira Ford is a talented singer at Reefside High School, during which time she has pursued singing records and performs gigs at the local coffee shop where most students go after school.

After her interaction with the yellow dino gem, Kira obtained the power of Ptera scream. This gave her the power to emit a sonic scream from her vocal cords with a destructive force able to causel physical damage to objects.

Dino Powers and Abilities
T-Rex Speed Due to the Red Dino Gem, Conner has the power to move and react at superhuman speeds. This includes his physical speed, reaction time, and reflexes. His maximum speed is unknown, but somewhere along the regions of the speed of sound. When using his powers, everything in normal motion appears to slow down to his perspective, while he accelerates to his opponent's.
Tricera Skin Due to the Blue Dino Gem, Ethan has the power to make his skin impenetrable and as hard as a rock, similar to the scales of a Triceratops. His skin turns scale-like and goes blue when he uses this power.
Ptera Scream Due to the powers of the Yellow Dino Gem, Kira is able to emit a destructive sonic scream from her vocal cords that has enough power to flatten anything in the scream's path. As a sound-based attack, it also has the added effect of harming the target's hearing.
